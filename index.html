The problem

The product gets engineered.
The revenue engine gets improvised.

When growth stalls or margins slip, the instinct is to blame the tooling — the CRM, the dashboards, the process. The real constraint almost always sits upstream: in pricing, packaging, pipeline design, forecasting, and the governance that holds them together. Those get treated as things you figure out later, once there's a team to run them. We treat them as the engine itself — sitting between the strategy firms that diagnose and leave and the RevOps shops that execute without commercial fluency. We find the commercial problem underneath, then stay and operate the engine we design.
